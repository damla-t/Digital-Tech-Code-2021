AS 91617 (3.10) Undertake a critique of a technological outcome's design - 3 Credits

Unlike most externals, this external is not an exam. It is a report that you need to write up.

You need to explain what a good design of your digital outcome would include. For example, in the case of a website you might talk about user Heuristics that would make a daily user communicate better etc., You need to explain the process of explaining a good website compared to a bad website. You might judge a digital outcome depending on sustainability, accessibility, functionality etc. A digital outcome can also be judged by the values and views held by the user.
