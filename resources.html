WHAT IS NCEA? SUBJECTS HOW TO NAIL EXAMS? STUDY TIPS RESOURCES

Here are some useful resources you can use to help you understand NCEA better.

NZQA The NZQA website can help you massively. You can log in to see your credits, as well as accessing past papers and getting information about NCEA.

Video explaining NCEAThis is the video that can be found in the 'What is NCEA?' page. It could help you understand NCEA better.

Study Time This website has some amazing resources for almost all internal and externals. Although it costs money if you can afford it, definitely worth it.

Youtube Channel explaining NCEA This is Study Time's Youtube Channel. They have some resources that show you how you should study for each subject, and not only that but they also have other videos that explain NCEA and University Entrance if you are interested in going to University.
